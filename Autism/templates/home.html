Treatment

Communication
Autism may impact verbal communication, leading to difficulties in expressing thoughts and feelings.
ADHD
Autism and ADHD commonly occur, and individuals with both conditions may experience challenges with attention, impulsivity, and hyperactivity, requiring tailored strategies and support.
Hearing
Individuals with autism may exhibit varying sensitivities to sounds, which can range from hypo- or hypersensitivity to certain auditory stimuli.
Sight
Some individuals with autism may have enhanced visual processing abilities, showcasing attention to detail and visual memory skills.
Touch
Sensory sensitivities in autism can extend to the sense of touch, with individuals often displaying heightened or reduced sensitivity to certain textures, clothing, or physical contact.
Educational
Individualized education plans (IEPs) play a vital role in supporting students with autism, ensuring tailored accommodations, strategies, and goals to address their unique learning needs.
